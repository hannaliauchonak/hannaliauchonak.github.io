Expert Elicitation Modeling

Turning expert estimates into probability distributions, scoring calibration, and aggregating judgments using principled weighting.

Goal

Convert L/M/H estimates into fitted distributions and combine expert opinions into an aggregated forecast.

Approach

Fit distributions (triangular/PERT/Beta/lognormal), evaluate calibration using Brier scoring, and weight experts using seed questions.

Deliverables

Individual + aggregate PDFs/plots, scoring summaries, and a workflow for comparing distributions across outcomes.

Key Components

  • Distribution fitting from expert L/M/H inputs
  • Calibration scoring (Brier) on seed variables
  • Expert weighting and aggregation
  • Visualization of uncertainty for decision support

Tools Used

Probability Brier Scoring Beta / PERT Uncertainty Modeling Visualization
